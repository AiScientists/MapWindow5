Get support

Do remember we are all volunteers on the MapWindow Project and we will try to help you as much as possible.
The best practice is if you have a question about MapWindow 5 you post a question on the Discussion page on codeplex. We will try to reply as soon as possible, mostly within 2-3 working days.
You can also consider getting a Premium Support Subscription. For $1,000 a year the core developers will reply you within 4 hours on working days. The money will be used for the development of MapWindow 5 and MapWinGIS.
